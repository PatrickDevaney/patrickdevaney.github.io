I am a Computer Science PhD student in the [ISD Lab](https://www.cs.ubc.ca/labs/isd/) at the University of British Columbia, supervised by [Alan Hu](https://www.cs.ubc.ca/~ajh/).
My primary areas of research interest are formal verification and theory of computation (and the interplay between these areas).

Prior to coming to UBC, I an MSc in Computer Science on approximation algorithms for computational geometry at the University of Windsor,
supervised by [Ahmad Biniaz](https://cglab.ca/~biniaz/) and [Prosenjit Bose](https://jitbose.ca/).

My [Erdős number](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Erd%C5%91s_number) is at most 3 (via, e.g., Erdős-Aronov-Bose-Devaney or Erdős-Shallit-Biniaz-Devaney).

Outside of academics, I enjoy reading, yoga, and nature photography.

Research Interests
======
- Theory of Computation: algorithms, data structures, complexity
- Discrete Math: combinatorial optimization, computational geometry, graph theory
- Formal Methods: verification of hardware and software, automated theorem proving, SAT for math, formalization of math
